About

People of Promise (POP) is a community development program that equips homeless and underemployed individuals with tools, skills and opportunity to start or participate in the operation of a micro-business.

By harnessing the potential and personal passions of each individual, POP serves as a people-centered approach towards addressing systemic problems of unemployment.

Support POP

POP is supported through generous individuals who give their time to mentor clients and/or give financially to sponsor a clients development.

Contact us to learn more about becoming a mentor or program sponsor.
